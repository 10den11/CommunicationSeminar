What is ComSem?

ComSem stands for Communications Seminar, a resource for students learning English and their teachers to improve your spoken English.

For Teachers

ComSem is a place for you to provide written and audio feedback for your students, after they have completed a fluency activity (such as Small Talk) or an oral presentation. The goal is to use corrective feedback to help your students develop accuracy, fluency, and complexity.

For Students

After your teacher provides feedback on things you have said that are not clear (or use the wrong grammar/vocabulary), you can test yourself on your sentences and see how much you have learned.

For Teachers/Researchers

The Archive provides a database of authentic language samples, all of which have been part-of- speech tagged and many of which have been manually error tagged. You can search and filter by grammatical feature, lexis, L1, proficiency level, and topic.
